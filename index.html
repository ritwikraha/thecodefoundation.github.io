Our Work

The Code Foundation is an open source organization focusing on developing cutting edge technological solutions which are in high demand of the community.

Some of the best softwares the world has seen, and are widely used have been developed as open source initiatives - Linux, MySQL, WordPress, WikiPedia, Firefox, the list goes on!

We are a group of highly enthusiastic people looking to make a wide impact in the world of information technology through collaborative efforts of the community, focused into projects which demand all levels of skills from noobs to legends. The community is free for all, ever welcoming to all who wish to code their way to a better world!
